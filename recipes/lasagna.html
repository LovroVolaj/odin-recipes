Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich sauces, cheeses, and often meats or vegetables.

Ingridients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan with onion, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning, then let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Spread a little sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
  6. Add a layer of meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan.
  7. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  8. Top with extra mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 35–45 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  10. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
