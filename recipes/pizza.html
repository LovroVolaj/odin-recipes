Pizza Recipe

Decription:

Pizza is an iconic, oven-baked dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened, leavened wheat dough base. It is traditionally topped with a vibrant, seasoned tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and various savory garnishes like herbs, meats, and vegetables.

Ingridients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape.
  3. Place the dough on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Add pepperoni or any toppings you like.
  7. Sprinkle oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper over the pizza.
  8. Drizzle a little olive oil on top.
  9. Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
